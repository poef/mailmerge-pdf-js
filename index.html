Mailmerge PDF proof of concept

This browser-only demo turns a deliberately small HTML template into one combined PDF. It uses pdf-lib directly instead of rendering the page to canvas.

1. Select people

2. Optional school logo

The template uses <img src="school-logo">. Uploading a PNG or JPEG here replaces the included demo logo.

3. Template

Supported tags in this proof of concept: h1, h2, p, br, strong, em, span, img, and <div class="page-break">.
